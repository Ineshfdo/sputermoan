About Spider-Man

Spider-Man is a superhero created by writer Stan Lee and writer-artist Steve Ditko. He first appeared in the comic book Amazing Fantasy #15 in 1962. Spider-Man is the alias of Peter Parker, an ordinary young man with extraordinary abilities, including superhuman strength, agility, and the ability to cling to walls.
